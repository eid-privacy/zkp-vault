# Comparing ZK Systems (eid-privacy, 2025)

## Summary
Comparative analysis of implemented ZKP systems for digital identity infrastructure. Categorises systems by family (hPKC, DLP, IP, MPC, ZK-STARK, IVC+hPKC) and evaluates prover resource efficiency, transparency, and post-quantum safety. Highlights Crescent and Longfellow as the two most promising approaches for ECDSA-signed credential use cases.
