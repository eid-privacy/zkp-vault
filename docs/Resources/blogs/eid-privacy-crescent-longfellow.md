# Crescent and Longfellow (eid-privacy, 2025)

## Summary
Side-by-side comparison of Microsoft Crescent and Google Longfellow as candidate ZKP systems for privacy-preserving e-ID credentials. Covers architecture, performance benchmarks (Crescent: ~1 s online after 20 s pre-computation; Longfellow: 1.17 s prover / 0.68 s verifier on Pixel 6 Pro, ~300 KB proof), setup requirements, post-quantum posture, and suitability for Swiss e-ID (Swiyu) deployment. Crescent relies on a trusted setup (Groth16) but has a smaller online proof; Longfellow is transparent and post-quantum safe but larger.
