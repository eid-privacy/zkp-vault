# Taxonomy 101 (eid-privacy, 2025)

## Summary
Introductory post for the WP1 Systematization of Knowledge paper on privacy-preserving electronic identities. Outlines the taxonomy framework: privacy goals and threat models, cryptographic building blocks, hardware security methods, data structures, regulatory frameworks, and a cross-evaluation methodology mapping technical solutions to regulatory requirements.
